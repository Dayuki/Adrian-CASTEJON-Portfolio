Portfolio

Here, you can find a bunch of projects that I worked on during past years.
Just click on a thumbnail to check a video of the game !

Psychedelink


Embark in a co-op exclusive 3D platformer game in a colorful and oniric world, built around a key feature : a link between players, both a constraint and a power. Psychedelink is my graduation project. It has beend developed by a team of 15 talented people from various disciplines.

Star Shaman


Go on a galactic journey to bring back life to the universe in this VR roguelite shooter ! Developed for the Oculust Quest by Ikimasho, where I worked for 10 months as an Unity integrator.

Super Chain Master


Super Chain Master is a mobile puzzle-game featuring RPG elements. Fight your way through in a puzzle-assembling turn based system ! Developed in a team during my 2nd year at Isart.

Umbra


In this metroidvania, helped by a strange spirit, you will explore the dark depths of a dungeon, accessing new areas as you unlock new abilities. Developed in a team during my 3rd year at Isart.

08:25


08:25 is a narrative experience in which you explore the house of a man who seems trapped in a time loop. As you wander, uncover the truth piece by piece. Your actions will impact your discovery. Developed in a team during my 2nd year at Isart.

Blood & Sand


Embarked at the back of car driving through the desert, shoot your chasers down and blow them away with your telekinetic powers in this VR arcade action game ! Developed in a team during a Game Week.

Mekagob


Explore the world with your team of goblins, searching for tools to acces new areas, and fight your way through in a turn-based RPG system. Developed in a team during my 3rd year at Isart.

About Me


I have graduated from a game design and programming formation at ISART Digital, a school specialized in video games.
Video games are my passion ever since I can remember and I've grown a desire to make my own for many years now.
I am highly motivated to make my contribution to the ever growing construction which is the video game industry.
I am by heart, training and experience a programmer and a designer, but I also like to do some graphic developement, mainly pixel art.
